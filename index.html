How to Play the Game

Welcome fearsome warrior! The Mandalorian and Boba Fett are occupied fighting against enemy stormtroopers. They will need your help to take care of Baby Yoda. Baby Yoda is a simple creature and does not require much. You just need to do 3 things

  • Feed Baby Yoda his yummy crickets
  • Turn off the lights in the ship so he can get some sleep
  • Cure his curiousity and boredom by letting him play with your lightsaber
    • You must click on the buttons in order to keep Baby Yoda well rested, his belly full, and his boredom cured. Every few seconds his metrics will decrease so make sure to keep an eye on this little one! As time passes, baby yoda will grow larger and stronger. Happy baby sitting! May the force be with you!
